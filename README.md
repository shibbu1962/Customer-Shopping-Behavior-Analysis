# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

End-to-end data analysis on **3,900 retail transactions** to uncover insights on revenue, loyalty, and customer engagement.

**Tools:** Python · PostgreSQL · Power BI

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

- 📈 **Subscribed customers** spend more on average and generate higher total revenue
- 🚚 **Express shipping users** are premium, high-value buyers
- 🏷️ **Discounts** are being given to customers who would buy anyway — a margin risk
- 👥 **Loyal customers** (10+ purchases) have significantly higher subscription rates
- ⭐ **Top-rated products:** Dress, Blouse, Sweater — ideal for marketing focus

---

## 💡 Recommendations

| Priority | Action |
|----------|--------|
| 🔴 High | Reduce blanket discounts — target by segment to protect margins |
| 🔴 High | Convert frequent buyers to subscribers with exclusive perks |
| 🟡 Medium | Launch loyalty rewards to accelerate repeat purchases |
| 🟢 Low | Focus marketing on high-revenue age groups & express shipping users |
